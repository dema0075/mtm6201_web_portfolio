Our Process

Domed sticker composition

Step by step

  • We create the design and shape of the required sticker or label.
  • We proceed to print the surface in full color in high resolution, we can print on most adhesive materials, we use eco-solvent and uv printing.
  • We use printing/cutting plotter, which allows us to make any size and shape.
  • The transparent polyurethane resin is then applied to the printed surface in the appropriate amount, thus achieving the desired volume.
  • Finally, drying proceeds, achieving a high-gloss product with an excellent three-dimensional finish.
